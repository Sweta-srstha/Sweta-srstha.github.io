Namaste, I'm Sweta.

A Product Designer, sunshine seeker, and storyteller at heart. Born in the hills of Nepal and now designing from the Bay Area, I bring curiosity, culture, and a splash of joy to everything I create. Whether I’m sketching wireframes or sipping masala chai while journaling design ideas, I always follow one principle: design should feel like a warm hug thoughtful, intuitive, and human.

With a Master’s in Computer Science , blending technical skills with human-centered thinking to create digital experiences that are not only functional, but truly feel good to use. Design is where my logical mind meets my artistic heart. I love digging into what makes people tick, listening deeply, and turning insights into clean, intuitive designs that make everyday moments smoother (and maybe a bit more joyful).

I believe in small moments of magic that thoughtful micro-interaction, that smooth user flow, that smile a user gets when something just works. That’s the kind of impact I want to create. And I’m here to keep learning, keep building, and keep creating experiences that connect.
